Competitions & Pathways

RobotLab provides students with opportunities to participate in local, national and international robotics competitions, helping them develop engineering, coding, teamwork and leadership skills.

Featured Competitions

Te Ara Hiko

New Zealand's robotics competition connecting students through innovation, teamwork and engineering challenges.

MakeX Challenge

Students design, build and program robots to solve real-world challenges through teamwork and creative problem-solving.

International Competitions

Outstanding teams can represent New Zealand at international robotics events and championships.

Why Compete?

Build Skills

Develop robotics, coding, engineering and critical-thinking skills through hands-on competition experiences.

Teamwork & Leadership

Learn collaboration, communication and leadership while working as part of a robotics team.

Global Opportunities

Top-performing students may travel internationally and compete on the world stage.

Benefits of Competition

Innovation

Students learn how to design creative engineering solutions to real-world problems.

Confidence

Competitions encourage students to present ideas, solve challenges and work under pressure.

Future Careers

Robotics competitions inspire future pathways into engineering, technology, AI and innovation industries.
