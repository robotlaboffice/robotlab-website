Competitions

RobotLab provides students with opportunities to compete, collaborate, innovate and represent New Zealand through exciting robotics competitions.

Featured Competitions

MakeX World Championship

Skills Developed Through Competition

🤖 Robotics Engineering

Design and build competition-ready robots.

💻 Coding & AI

Program robots to complete challenges and tasks.

🤝 Teamwork

Work effectively with teammates under competition conditions.

Why Compete?

Competitions help students build confidence, develop leadership skills, solve complex problems and gain valuable real-world STEM experience. They also create opportunities for international travel, networking and future career pathways.
