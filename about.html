About RobotLab

RobotLab is a robotics club and STEM learning community helping students explore robotics, coding, engineering and artificial intelligence through hands-on learning, teamwork and competition.

Our Mission

To inspire young people to become innovators, problem-solvers and future technology leaders through robotics, coding and engineering experiences.

Our Vision

To build New Zealand's leading robotics club community, creating pathways from classroom learning to national competitions, international events and future STEM careers.

Why Robotics Matters

Robotics combines coding, engineering, creativity and teamwork into one exciting learning experience. Through robotics, students develop confidence, problem-solving skills and the ability to turn ideas into real-world solutions.



At RobotLab, we believe every student should have the opportunity to explore technology, innovation and future careers through hands-on learning.
