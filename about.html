About RobotLab

RobotLab is a robotics club and STEM learning community helping students explore robotics, coding, engineering and artificial intelligence through hands-on learning, teamwork and competition.

Our Mission

To inspire young people to become innovators, problem-solvers and future technology leaders through robotics, coding and engineering experiences.

Our Vision

To build New Zealand's leading robotics club community, creating pathways from classroom learning to national competitions, international events and future STEM careers.

What We Do

School Programmes

Robotics clubs and STEAM programmes delivered in schools.

Holiday Camps

Fun and engaging robotics, coding and AI camps during school holidays.

Teacher Training

Supporting educators with robotics and technology learning programmes.

Competition Pathways

Te Ara Hiko

New Zealand's robotics competition connecting students through innovation and teamwork.

MakeX Challenge

International robotics competitions where students solve engineering challenges.

Global Opportunities

Outstanding students may represent New Zealand internationally.
