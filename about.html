About RobotLab

RobotLab is a New Zealand-based robotics, coding and artificial intelligence education provider. We help students develop future-ready skills through hands-on learning experiences, school programmes, holiday camps and international robotics competitions.

Our Mission

To make robotics, coding, engineering and AI education accessible to students across New Zealand, empowering the next generation of innovators, problem-solvers and technology leaders.

Our Vision

To become New Zealand's leading robotics education ecosystem, creating pathways from classroom learning to national and international competitions, higher education and future technology careers.

What We Do

School Programmes

Curriculum-aligned robotics and STEAM programmes delivered in schools.

Holiday Camps

Hands-on robotics, coding and AI learning experiences during school holidays.

Teacher Training

Professional development workshops supporting educators and schools.

Competitions & Pathways

Te Ara Hiko

A New Zealand robotics competition connecting students through innovation and teamwork.

MakeX Challenge

International robotics competitions where students solve real-world engineering challenges.

Global Opportunities

Outstanding teams can represent New Zealand in international robotics competitions and events.

Our Partners

PB Tech • Waka Pacific Trust • Due Drop Centre • Schools Across New Zealand

Our Impact

RobotLab works with schools, communities and industry partners to increase access to robotics, engineering and AI education. Through competitions, workshops and learning programmes, students gain practical skills, confidence and pathways into future technology careers.
