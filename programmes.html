Our Programmes

RobotLab provides engaging robotics, coding, AI and STEAM programmes for students, teachers and schools across New Zealand.

After School Robotics Clubs

Weekly robotics and coding programmes delivered in schools. Students learn engineering, programming and problem-solving skills through hands-on projects.

Holiday Camps

Fun and engaging STEAM learning experiences during school holidays. Students explore robotics, coding, AI and technology through practical activities.

Teacher Training

Professional development workshops that help teachers confidently deliver robotics and technology programmes in their schools.

Why Choose RobotLab?

Hands-On Learning

Students learn by building, programming and solving real-world challenges.

Competition Pathways

Opportunities to participate in MakeX, Te Ara Hiko and international robotics competitions.

Future Skills

Develop creativity, teamwork, coding, engineering and AI skills for the future.
